Our Story

A place to slow
down and savor.

Tucked into a quiet plaza on East Live Oak Avenue, Cafe Sweets is a family-run café where every drink is brewed to order and every bite is made with care. We're known for our delicate Almond Tea Cakes, signature Crème Brûlée Latte, and the kind of warm, unhurried hospitality that keeps people coming back.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick matcha, settling in for a bowl of noodles, or challenging a friend to mahjong — this is your place.
